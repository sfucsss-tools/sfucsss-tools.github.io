Tentative Grant Request


This is an abridged version of the the SFSS Online Grant Submission Webform.


Instructions:

  1. Fill out the form

  2. Send the resultant PDF to your treasurer

Prerequisites:

  1. (SU only) Ensure that a motion for this grant has been made at a general meeting, and you have access to the meeting minutes
    2.   Format: <name> motions <$x> from core for a grant of up to <$total> for <description>

  2. Make sure the space / room has already been booked (this is not strictly necessary, but increases chances)

  3. Ensure your grant request is reasonable, and at least roughly follows the SFSS Funding 101 Guidelines

  4. Ensure this form is filled out at least
    11 business days (> 15 days) before your event for grants of up to $1000, and
    22 business days (> 30 days) for grants over $1000


Note: This form saves your responses as you input them!
